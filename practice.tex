(62) \newline
$ k = 1.13 \times 10^3 \frac{\text{N}}{\text{m}} $ \newline
$ m = 1.67 \times 10^{-27} \text{ kg} $ \newline

a. Find f
$$ w = \sqrt{\frac{k}{m}} = 2\pi f $$
$$ f = \frac{\sqrt{\frac{k}{m}}}{2\pi} $$
$$ f = \frac{\sqrt{\frac{\left(1.13 \times 10^3 \frac{\text{N}}{\text{m}}\right)}{\left(1.67 \times 10^{-27} \text{ kg} \div 2\right)}}}{2\pi} $$
$$ f = 1.851 \times 10^{14} \text{ Hz}$$

b. Find $E$ and $\lambda$
$$ \Delta E_{43} = hf $$
$$ \Delta E_{43} = \left(6.626 \times 10^{-34} \text{ Js}\right) \left(1.851 \times 10^{14} \text{ Hz}\right)$$
$$ \Delta E_{43} = 1.227 \times 10^{-19} \text{ J}$$

$$ \lambda = \frac{v}{f} $$
$$ \lambda = \frac{3.00 \times 10^8 \frac{\text{m}}{\text{s}}}{1.851 \times 10^{14} \text{ Hz}} $$
$$ \lambda = 1.621 \times 10^{-6} \text{ m} $$
