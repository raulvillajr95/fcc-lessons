(74) Probability at diff K \newline
$ m = 6.64 \times 10^{-27} \text{ kg} $ \newline
$ L = 2.0 \times 10^{-15} \text{ m} $ \newline
$ U_0 = 30.0 \times 10^6 \text{ eV} \times \frac{1.602 \times 10^{-19} \text{ J}}{1 \text{ eV}} = 4.806 \times 10^{-12} \text{ J}$ \newline

$
\beta = \sqrt{\frac{2m\left(U_0 - E\right)}{\hbar^2}}
$ \newline

$
k = \sqrt{\frac{2mE}{\hbar^2}}
$ \newline

$
\gamma = \frac{\beta}{k} - \frac{k}{\beta}
$ \newline

$
P = \frac{1}{\cosh^2\left(\beta L\right) + \left(\frac{\gamma}{2}\right)^2\sinh^2\left(\beta L\right)}
$ \newline

When $ K = 29.0 \text{ MeV} $ \newline
$ E = 29.0 \times 10^6 \text{ eV} \times \frac{1.602 \times 10^{-19} \text{ J}}{1 \text{ eV}} = 4.646 \times 10^{-12} \text{ J}$ \newline

$$
\beta = \sqrt{\frac{2\left(6.64 \times 10^{-27} \text{ kg}\right)\left(4.806 \times 10^{-12} \text{ J} - 4.646 \times 10^{-12} \text{ J}\right)}{\left(\frac{6.626 \times 10^{-34} \text{J s}}{2\pi}\right)^2}}
= 4.371 \times 10^{14}
$$

When $ K = 20.0 \text{ MeV} $ \newline
$ E = 20.0 \times 10^6 \text{ eV} \times \frac{1.602 \times 10^{-19} \text{ J}}{1 \text{ eV}} = 3.204 \times 10^{-12} \text{ J}$ \newline
