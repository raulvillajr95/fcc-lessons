Que) Work \newline

A $\to$ B \newline
$
W = P_i\Delta V
= 5.40\times 10^5 \text{ Pa} \times
\left(0.451 - 0.272\right) \text{ m}^3
= 96660 \text{ J}
$ \newline

B $\to$ C \newline
$
W = \frac{P_i V_i - P_f V_f}{\gamma - 1}
= \frac{5.40\times 10^5 \text{ Pa} 0.451\text{ m}^3 - 
  1.55\times 10^5 \text{ Pa} 0.950\text{ m}^3}
  {1 + \frac{2}{3} - 1}
= 144435 \text{ J}
$ \newline

C $\to$ A \newline
$
W = P_i V_i \ln\left(\frac{V_f}{V_i}\right)
= 1.55\times 10^5 \text{ Pa} 0.950\text{ m}^3
  \ln\left(\frac{0.272\text{ m}^3}{0.950\text{ m}^3}\right)
= -184160 \text{ J}
$ \newline

$W_{net}$ \newline
$
W_{net} = \left(96660 + 144435 - 184160\right)
  \text{ J}
= 56935 \text{ J}
$ \newline
