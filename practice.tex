Build $ -\frac{\hbar^2}{2m}\frac{d^2}{dx^2}\psi(x) $
$$ \psi_0'(x) = \left(\frac{\beta^2}{\pi}\right)^{\frac{1}{4}} e^{-\frac{1}{2} \beta^2 x^2} \left(- \beta^2 x\right) $$
$$ \psi_0''(x) = \left(\frac{\beta^2}{\pi}\right)^{\frac{1}{4}} \left[ \left(e^{-\frac{1}{2} \beta^2 x^2} \times -\beta^2\right) + \left(e^{-\frac{1}{2} \beta^2 x^2} \left(- \beta^2 x\right) \times -\beta^2 x\right) \right]$$
$$ = \left(\frac{\beta^2}{\pi}\right)^{\frac{1}{4}} e^{-\frac{1}{2} \beta^2 x^2} \left[\left(- \beta^2\right) + \beta^4 x^2\right]$$
$$ = \left(\frac{\beta^2}{\pi}\right)^{\frac{1}{4}} e^{-\frac{1}{2} \beta^2 x^2} \left(\beta^2\right)\left[\beta^2 x^2 - 1\right]$$

$$ -\frac{\hbar^2}{2m} \frac{d^2}{dx^2}\psi(x) = \left(-\frac{\hbar^2}{2m}\right) \left(\left(\frac{\beta^2}{\pi}\right)^{\frac{1}{4}} e^{-\frac{1}{2} \beta^2 x^2} \left(\beta^2\right)\left[\beta^2 x^2 - 1\right] \right) $$

Build $ U(x)\psi (x) $
$$ U(x)\psi (x) = \left(\frac{mw^2 x^2}{2}\right) \left(\frac{\beta^2}{\pi}\right)^{\frac{1}{4}} e^{-\frac{1}{2} \beta^2 x^2} $$

Build $E \psi (x)$
$$ E_0 = \frac{\hbar \omega}{2} $$
$$ E_0\psi (x) = \frac{\hbar \omega}{2} \left(\frac{\beta^2}{\pi}\right)^{\frac{1}{4}} e^{-\frac{1}{2} \beta^2 x^2}$$
