(27) \newline
$ \lambda = 589 \times 10^{-9} \text{ m} $ \newline
$ t = 1.60 \times 10^{-8} \text{ s} $ \newline
$ m = 9.11 \times 10^{-31} \text{ kg} $ \newline

a. Find the $\Delta E$ \newline
$
\Delta E \Delta t \geq \frac{\hbar}{2} \text{ ; }
\frac{h}{4\pi}
$ \newline
$
\Delta E \geq \frac{h}{4\pi \Delta t} \text{ ; }
\frac{6.626 \times 10^{-34} \text{ J s}}
{4\pi \times 1.60 \times 10^{-8} \text{ s}}
= 3.296 \times 10^{-27} \text{ J}
\left(
  \frac{1 \text{ eV}}{1.602 \times 10^{-19} \text{ J}}
\right) 
$ \newline
$
\Delta E \geq 2.057 \times 10^{-8} \text{ eV}
$ \newline

b. Find $f$ \newline
$ 
f = \frac{v}{\lambda}
= \frac{3.00 \times 10^8 \frac{\text{m}}{\text{s}}}
  {589 \times 10^{-9} \text{ m}}
= 5.093 \times 10^{14} \text{ Hz}
$ \newline

c. Find $\Delta f$ \newline
$
\Delta E = h\Delta f \text{ ; }
\Delta f = \frac{\Delta E}{h}
= \frac{3.296 \times 10^{-27} \text{ J}}
  {6.626 \times 10^{-34} \text{ J s}}
= 4.974 \times 10^6 \text{ Hz}
$ \newline
$
\% = \frac{4.974 \times 10^6 \text{ Hz}}
  {5.093 \times 10^{14} \text{ Hz}} \times 100
= 9.766 \times 10^{-7} \%
$ \newline
