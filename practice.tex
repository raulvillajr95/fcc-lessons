(4) d. From $ n = 4 $ to $ n = 1 $ \newline
Find energy (eV) \newline 
$$ E_p = E_4 - E_1 $$
$$ E_n = \frac{\pi^2 \hbar^2}{2mL^2} n^2 $$
$$ E_n = \frac{\left(\pi\hbar\right)^2}{2mL^2} n^2 $$
$$ E_n = \frac{\left(\pi\frac{h}{2\pi}\right)^2}{2mL^2} n^2 $$
$$ E_n = \frac{\left(\frac{h}{2}\right)^2}{2mL^2} n^2 $$
$$ E_n = \frac{h^2}{8mL^2} n^2 $$
$$ E_p = \frac{h^2}{8mL^2} 4^2 - \frac{h^2}{8mL^2} 1^2 $$
$$ E_p = \frac{h^2}{8mL^2} \left(4^2 - 1^2\right)$$
$$ E_p = \frac{h^2}{8mL^2} \left(15\right)$$
$$ E_p = \frac{\left(6.626 \times 10^{-34} \text{J s}\right)^2}{8\left(1.67 \times 10^{-27} \text{kg}\right)\left(1.733 \times 10^{-11}\right)^2} \left(15\right)$$
$$ E_p = 1.094 \times 10^{-19} \text{J} $$
$$ E_p = 1.094 \times 10^{-19} \text{J}\left(\frac{1 \text{eV}}{1.602 \times 10^{-19} \text{J}}\right) = 0.6829 \text{ eV}$$

Find frequency (Hz)
$$ f = \frac{E}{h} $$
$$ f = \frac{1.094 \times 10^{-19} \text{J}}{6.626 \times 10^{-34} \text{J s}} = 1.651 \times 10^{14} \text{ Hz} $$

Find wavelength
$$ \lambda = \frac{v}{f} $$
$$ \lambda = \frac{3.00 \times 10^8 \frac{\text{m}}{\text{s}}}{1.651 \times 10^{14} \text{ Hz}} = 1.817 \times 10^{-6} \text{m} $$
