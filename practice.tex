(4) d. From $ n = 4 $ to $ n = 1 $ \newline
Find energy (eV) \newline 
$$ E_n = \left(n + \frac{1}{2}\right)\hbar\omega $$
$$ E_n = \left(n + \frac{1}{2}\right)\left(\frac{h}{2\pi}\right)\left(2\pi f\right) $$
$$ E_n = \left(n + \frac{1}{2}\right)hf$$
$$ E_n = \left(n_a + \frac{1}{2}\right)hf - \left(n_b + \frac{1}{2}\right)hf$$
$$ E_n = \left(n_a - n_b\right)hf$$
$$ E_1 = 13.6 \text{ eV} $$
$$ E_p = |E_f - E_i| $$
$$ E_p = \left|\frac{-13.6 \text{eV}}{1^2} - \frac{-13.6 \text{eV}}{4^2}\right| = 12.75 \text{ eV}$$
$$ E_p = 12.75 \text{ eV}\left(\frac{1.602 \times 10^{-19} \text{J}}{1 \text{eV}}\right) = 2.043 \times 10^{-18} \text{ J}$$

Find frequency (Hz)
$$ f = \frac{E}{h} $$
$$ f = \frac{2.043 \times 10^{-18} \text{ J}}{6.626 \times 10^{-34} \text{J s}} = 3.083 \times 10^{15} \text{ Hz} $$

Find wavelength
$$ \lambda = \frac{v}{f} $$
$$ \lambda = \frac{3.00 \times 10^8 \frac{\text{m}}{\text{s}}}{3.083 \times 10^{15} \text{ Hz}} = 9.732 \times 10^{-8} \text{m} $$
