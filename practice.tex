(3) Isobaric Process \newline
$ n = 0.475 \text{ mol} $ \newline
$ T_i = 575 \text{ K} $ \newline
$ T_f = 1150 \text{ K} $ \newline
$ f_{\le 1150 \text{ K}} = 5 $ \newline
$ f_{> 1150 \text{ K}} = 7 $ \newline

a. Total Q \newline
$
Q = n C_p \Delta T
$ \newline
$
C_p = \left(\frac{f}{2} + 1\right)R
$ \newline

From $ 575 $ K to $ 1150 $ K \newline
$
C_p = \left(\frac{5}{2} + 1\right)R = \frac{7}{2}R
= \frac{7}{2} 8.314 \frac{\text{J}}{\text{K mol}}
= 29.1 \frac{\text{J}}{\text{K mol}}
$ \newline
$
Q = 0.475 \text{ mol} \times
29.1 \frac{\text{J}}{\text{K mol}} \times
\left(1150 \text{ K} - 575 \text{ K}\right)
= 7948 \text{ J}
$ \newline

From $ 1151 $ K to $ 1575 $ K \newline
$
C_p = \left(\frac{7}{2} + 1\right)R = \frac{9}{2}R
= \frac{9}{2} 8.314 \frac{\text{J}}{\text{K mol}}
= 37.41 \frac{\text{J}}{\text{K mol}}
$\newline
$
Q = 0.475 \text{ mol} \times
37.41 \frac{\text{J}}{\text{K mol}} \times
\left(1575 \text{ K} - 1151 \text{ K}\right)
= 7534 \text{ J}
$ \newline

$
Q_{tot} = \left(7948 + 7534\right) \text{ J}
= 15482 \text{ J}
$ \newline

b. Total $ W $ \newline
$
W = Q - \Delta E_{int}
$ \newline
$
W_{tot} = \left(15482 - 11539\right) \text{ J}
= 3943 \text{ J}
$ \newline

c. Total $ \Delta E_{int} $ \newline
$
\Delta E_{int} = n C_v \Delta T
$ \newline
$
C_v = \frac{f}{2}R
$ \newline

From $ 575 $ K to $ 1150 $ K \newline
$
C_v = \frac{5}{2}R 
= \frac{5}{2} 8.314 \frac{\text{J}}{\text{K mol}}
= 20.79 \frac{\text{J}}{\text{K mol}}
$ \newline
$
\Delta E_{int} = 0.475 \text{ mol} \times
20.79 \frac{\text{J}}{\text{K mol}} \times
\left(1150 \text{ K} - 575 \text{ K}\right)
= 5678 \text{ J}
$ \newline

From $ 1151 $ K to $ 1575 $ K \newline
$
C_v = \frac{7}{2}R 
= \frac{7}{2} 8.314 \frac{\text{J}}{\text{K mol}}
= 29.1 \frac{\text{J}}{\text{K mol}}
$ \newline
$
\Delta E_{int} = 0.475 \text{ mol} \times
29.1 \frac{\text{J}}{\text{K mol}} \times
\left(1575 \text{ K} - 1151 \text{ K}\right)
= 5861 \text{ J}
$ \newline
$
\Delta E_{int tot} = \left(5678 + 5861\right) \text{ J}
= 11539 \text{ J}
$ \newline
