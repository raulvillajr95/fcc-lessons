Quiz Practice \newline
Calculate probabilites and expectation values. \newline

$\psi(x) = C\left(1 - x^2\right)$ \newline
for $0 \leq x \leq 1$ \newline
$= 0$ everywhere else \newline

$
\int \left|\psi(x)\right|^2 dx
$ \newline
$
\int \left|C\left(1 - x^2\right)\right|^2 dx
$ \newline
$
C^2 \int \left(1 - x^2\right)^2 dx
$ \newline
$
C^2 \int \left(1 - 2x^2 + x^4\right) dx
$ \newline
$
C^2 \left[x - \frac{2x^3}{3} + \frac{x^5}{5}\right]
$ \newline

Find normalization \newline
$
C^2 \left[x - \frac{2x^3}{3} + \frac{x^5}{5}\right]_0^1
= 1
$ \newline
$
C^2 \left[
    \left(1 - \frac{2\times1^3}{3} + \frac{1^5}{5}\right) -
    \left(0 - \frac{2\times0^3}{3} + \frac{0^5}{5}\right)
  \right]
= 1
$ \newline
$
C^2 \left[
    \left(1 - \frac{2}{3} + \frac{1}{5}\right) -
    \left(0\right)
  \right]
= 1
$ \newline
$
C^2 \left(\frac{8}{15}\right)
= 1
$ \newline
$
C^2 = \frac{15}{8}
$ \newline
$
C = \sqrt{\frac{15}{8}}
$ \newline

Find expected pos \newline
$
\int x\left|\psi(x)\right|^2 dx
$ \newline
$
\int x\left|C\left(1 - x^2\right)\right|^2 dx
$ \newline
$
C^2 \int x\left(1 - x^2\right)^2 dx
$ \newline
$
C^2 \int x\left(1 - 2x^2 + x^4\right) dx
$ \newline
$
C^2 \int \left(x - 2x^3 + x^5\right) dx
$ \newline
$
C^2 \left[\frac{x^2}{2} - \frac{2x^4}{4} + \frac{x^6}{6}\right]
$ \newline
$
C^2 \left[\frac{x^2}{2} - \frac{x^4}{2} + \frac{x^6}{6}\right]
$ \newline
$
\frac{\frac{15}{8}}{2} \left[x^2 - x^4 + \frac{x^6}{3}\right]_0^1
$ \newline
$
\frac{15}{16} \left[
  \left(1^2 - 1^4 + \frac{1^6}{3}\right) -
  \left(0^2 - 0^4 + \frac{0^6}{3}\right)
  \right]
$ \newline
$
\frac{15}{16} \left[
  \left(1 - 1 + \frac{1}{3}\right) -
  \left(0\right)
  \right]
$ \newline
$
\frac{15}{16} \left(\frac{1}{3}\right)
= \frac{5}{16}
$ \newline
